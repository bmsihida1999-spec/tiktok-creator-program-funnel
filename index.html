Frequently Asked Questions

How and when do I get paid?

We process payments between the 15th and 20th of every month, directly after TikTok pays out. We support Wise, PayPal, Payoneer, and Crypto (USDT).

Do I need to show my face?

No. Our strategy focuses on "Faceless" content (e.g., documentary style, quizzes, stories). You just need great editing skills.

Is this a long-term position?

Yes. As the account grows, we offer a Long-Term Contract to secure your position. Additionally, you will have the option to buy equity (shares) in the account, or sell your accumulated shares back to us for a lump sum.

What if I'm a beginner?

We prefer experience with editing software (CapCut/Premiere), but if you have a strong portfolio and willingness to learn our hook strategies, we are open to training the right person.

How many videos do I need to edit per week?

We require 2 videos per day (approx. 14/week). Why? Because TikTok rewards daily consistency. We provide the scripts and assets so you can maintain this pace easily.

Do I have to write the scripts?

No. We provide the viral hooks, scripts, and professional AI/Human voiceovers. Your job is to bring the story to life with visuals, not to write it.

Is my test video safe?

Yes. We explicitly ask you to add a large WATERMARK to your test edit. This ensures we cannot use your work without paying you.

What happens if a video flops?

It happens to the best creators. We review the performance together on our Weekly Live Call, analyze the retention graph, fix the hook, and improve. We don't quit after one bad video.
